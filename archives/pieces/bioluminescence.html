bioluminescence

18 December 2024

my soul believed ] my heart knew
waves crash down ] truth they soar
mighty hope unburdened ] pulled down
outside reality ] inside gravity

try ] always the same
yet
try ] every ending a new beginning

Rise!

sometimes in not so summer
friend comes to visit
they are millions and
invisible except when
i move to fly they
come alive alight

dare to catch you
only in glimpses
your scarcity only
makes you brighter

a mother alone ] walk on beach with
watches you ] me with sand
shoes off hair floating ] squished in wet crocs
in soft wind looking ] look behind you and
in your direction ] see city look
behind those eyes ] above you and see
thoughts have gone ] stars red stars maybe
for a walk searching ] planes in sky see the pier
for a fire that went ] the brightest thing of all
out years ago ] i had been there once twice
but we are old now i think a stupid
thing to say but we feel it anyway

Rise!

soon i will leave
you warm ocean
of beautiful people
and return to
where i belong i
had hoped to ] had other plans
belong here ] waiting for me

so walk with
me one last
time form a
hand ] five fingers

Rise!

reach for mine
with yours and make a
promise that we will see each
other one day and it will be just as
it had always been ] if time had never passed
i will change however ] you will stay the same
i will live then die ] you will stay the same
i will take myself places ] you will stay here
we will return to the same spot
again and again and
again and
again
and
