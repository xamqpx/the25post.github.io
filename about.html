ABOUT

Content on The 25 Post is written (unless otherwise indicated), edited, and managed by Jonathan Park.

About the editor

Jonathan Park is a student at the University of Southern California, majoring in applied mathematics and minoring in computer science with a specialization in cybersecurity. He has been a staff news writer for the Daily Trojan, USC's student newspaper, since January 2022. He grew up in the Bay Area, California, and South Korea, interchangeably. He now lives in Orange County.

Besides writing and politics, he has interests in computer engineering, programming languages, music (nu jazz, hiphop, rhythm & blues, noise, classical), and reading.

You can find him on Instagram, GitHub, Spotify, and LinkedIn.

About the site

The 25 Post began as a writing account on Instagram, which went by many names, posting short pieces of 200-300 words. "Nelson" and "half moon bay" were originally posted to that Instagram account. Soon enough, the idea arose to expand the account into a dedicated website, which came to fruition as The 25 Post (named as such for the editor's year of expected graduation, 2025).

When it first launched on 7 April 2022, the Post was hosted on Google Sites. However, the need for further flexibility with customizations and aesthetics motivated the website's migration in early August 2022 to GitHub Pages, which hosts the repository of HTML, CSS, and related files which comprise the Post's website. You can view the site's GitHub repository here.
